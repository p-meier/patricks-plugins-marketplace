## Standard Report Template

```markdown
# {Title}

**Research Question**: {original question}
**Research Type**: {type}
**Date**: {date}
**Mode**: {Deep Project|Quick Session}

---

## Executive Summary
{3-5 bullet points for quick overview}

- {Core statement 1}
- {Core statement 2}
- {Core statement 3}
- {Recommendation}

---

## Context & Background
{1-2 paragraphs: Why is this topic relevant? What triggered the research?}

---

## Methodology
- **Sources**: {count} sources analyzed
- **Types**: {Reports, News, Reviews, etc.}
- **Timeframe**: {research period}

---

## Key Findings

### Finding 1: {Title}
{Detailed description}

**Evidence:**
- {Source 1}: {proof}
- {Source 2}: {proof}

**Implication:** {What does this mean?}

### Finding 2: {Title}
...

---

## Recommendations

### Recommendation 1: {Short title}
**What**: {Concrete action}
**Why**: {Reasoning from findings}
**Priority**: {High|Medium|Low}

---

## Open Questions
- {What could not be conclusively answered}
- {What needs further research}

---

## Sources
[1] {Author/Source}: {Title}. {URL}. {Date}.
[2] ...

---

*Created: {date} | Research Type: {type} | Mode: {mode}*
```

## Deep Report Template

```markdown
# {Title}

**Research Question**: {original question}
**Research Type**: {type}
**Date**: {date}
**Commissioned by**: {who requested}

---

## Executive Summary

### At a Glance
{3-5 bullet points for C-level}

### Core Recommendation
{1 sentence: The most important action recommendation}

### Decision-Relevant Numbers
| Metric | Value | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| {Metric 1} | {Value} | {Source} |
| {Metric 2} | {Value} | {Source} |

---

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Background
{Why this research? Context and triggers}

### 1.2 Research Questions
1. {Main question}
2. {Sub-question 1}
3. {Sub-question 2}

### 1.3 Scope & Limitations
- **Included**: {What was examined}
- **Excluded**: {What was intentionally not examined}
- **Limitations**: {Methodological constraints}

---

## 2. Methodology

### 2.1 Research Approach
{Description of process}

### 2.2 Source Overview
| Category | Count | Examples |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Reports | {n} | McKinsey, Gartner |
| News | {n} | TechCrunch, etc. |
| Reviews | {n} | G2, Capterra |
| Primary Sources | {n} | Documentation, etc. |

### 2.3 Quality Assurance
{How quality was ensured}

---

## 3. Meta-Principles

### Principle 1: {Short title}
**Statement**: {Clear statement in one sentence}
**Explanation**: {2-3 sentences context}
**Evidence**: {3+ examples}
**Implication**: {Practical meaning}

---

## 4. Detailed Findings

### 4.1 {Topic Area 1}
#### Status Quo
#### Developments
#### Evidence
#### Interpretation

---

## 5. Synthesis & Analysis

### 5.1 Cross-Cutting Patterns
### 5.2 Contradictions & Resolution
### 5.3 Validated Theses

---

## 6. Recommendations

### 6.1 Immediate Actions (0-3 months)
### 6.2 Medium-Term Actions (3-12 months)
### 6.3 Long-Term Direction

---

## 7. Risks & Uncertainties

### 7.1 Identified Risks
| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| {R1} | {H/M/L} | {H/M/L} | {Action} |

### 7.2 Open Questions
### 7.3 Recommended Follow-Up Research

---

## Appendix

### A. Complete Source List
### B. Glossary
### C. Thesis Log

---

*Created: {date} | Research Type: {type}*
```
